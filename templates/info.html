Info

So you probably have a few questions. Thats cool. Here is some answers to those questions.


Can I upload my current work?

I mean you can, I can't physically stop you. If I catch it I will remove it and report it to teachers though, so up to you.


Will my work get plagurised?

Nope. Since all work goes through plagscan, Any stolen work will be caught as soon as they submit. It doesn't make stealing work easier, it just makes it easier to find stuff to steal. If anything, it'll make it harder since teachers will be untrusting of my site and check your work even closer.


Will I get viruses?

I have done my best to block uploads of any dangerous files, but I can't block everything. Please check any PDFs or DOCX files you download before opening them.


What are the rules regarding earning a lollipop?

The bug must cause a 500 error page
You can't earn more than one lollipop for the same bug
You can't get the same bug as someone else (First to report it gets the lollipop)
You have to tell me what you did to get it

Can I change my name?

Yep. I can see who changes their names though so don't do anything offensive. The logic behind it is so that people don't have to use their deadnames on the site if they don't want to. Go to this page to change it. Please don't abuse it, or it will be removed.
