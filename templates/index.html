ASMShare

I got sick of 1 exemplar per assignment, so I made this

Assignment sharing for ASMS students

Fast, effective and anyonmous sharing of assignments

Safe

Uses SSL technology: The same stuff your bank's website runs on. It is also further encrypted cos I was bored on a friday afternoon. Also since the login is with google only, I never see any passwords

Anyonmous

The only details recorded is your name so the site can welcome you and an email address and that is only used so I can work out who uploaded 4 hours of "Around the world". Your email is never shown to anyone else.

Stylish design

I hate light mode as much as you guys, so thats why I didn't add it. Your eyes are safe from flashbangs. I also spent ages making sure it look nice and snazzy, on my laptop at least. I have no idea what it looks like for you.

Fast

The site squishes the files down to tenny tiny sizes, so you can get that F- Order from Chaos assignment really fast

More than just a cheat site assignment repository

Frequently asked questions

Answers to most common questions.

Do you have my password?

Nope. Google does though. Through some funky web magic, you sign in with Google and then Google just gives me your name and email. Your password is safe.

Uhhh I didn't know what to do for my industrial design assignment

What can I upload?

You can upload any file type you want, but PDF files are prefered

So I don't get arrested, you can't upload an assignment during the year you completed it. You will be able to upload it now and the assignment will appear on the site on the first of January
