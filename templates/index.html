Frequently asked questions

Answers to most common questions.

Do you have my password?

Nope. Google does though. Through some funky web magic, you sign in with Google and then Google just gives me your name and email. Your password is safe. And besides, I probably should not be trusted with your passwords since I have next to no understanding how hashing works.

Why did you make this?

Uhhh I didn't know what to do for my industrial design assignment. Also I wanted to see if I could make a site. Spoiler alert, I can.

What can I upload?

You can upload any file type you want, but PDF files are prefered cos I can turn them into nice images

Why didn't my upload appear?

So I don't get arrested by SACE moderators, you can't upload an assignment during the year you completed it. You will be able to upload it now and the assignment will appear on the site on the first of January

How did you make this?

Python, HTML and obscenly little sleep

How can I contact you?

At school I'm the dude with neon blue hair. Otherwise, you can find my socials n stuff here?
