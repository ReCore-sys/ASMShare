HTTP errors

So, you may be a little confused about what different errors means. Good news is that I am here to explain. I will explain the technical details, and also a version in the context of getting a drink from a bar


Errors 2xx


200

Everything went as planned. The page loaded and nothing broke. You probably won't see this one
You asked for a beer and the bartender said sure


201

You got the page back, but it had to be created to do so
You asked for a beer and the bartender said sure, but had to summon it out of the void


204

It worked, but you got nothing back. It didn't exactly break though
You ask for a beer and the bartender says sure. He hands you a glass of water, but you don't mind


Errors 3xx


301

The resource exists but has moved
You ask for a beer and the bartender says they don't sell it. He does however point you to a nearby bar that does


302

The resource exists but has moved. Future changes to the location may happen
You ask for a beer and the bartender says they don't sell it. He does however point you to a nearby bar that does. This bar happens to randomly teleport around town, so the bartender tells you to ask him where it is each time.

Errors 4xx


401

You need to sign in
You ask for a beer. The bartender asks for some ID.


403

You are not allowed to see this resource
You ask for a beer. The bartender reminds you of the many threats of physical violence you issued last week while drunk and kicks you out.


404

The resource does not exist
You ask for a beer. The bartender asks what a beer is.


405

That method is not allowed
You ask for a beer. The bartender does not speak English.


408

The resource timed out
You ask for a beer. The bartender takes too long to grab the beer so you leave.


410

The resource is gone with no further explaination
You ask for a beer. The bartender says no and refuses to elaborate.


429

You made too many requests too fast
You ask for a beer. The bartender says no because you have already had 86 that night.


451

This resource is blocked for legal reasons
You ask for a beer. It is 1930 however, so alcohol is illegal and you get shot by the police


Errors 5xx


500

The server breaks
You ask for a beer. The bartender has a hemorrhage.


502

The server can't fetch a resource
You ask for a beer. The bartender's hand melts off, then he apolgises.
